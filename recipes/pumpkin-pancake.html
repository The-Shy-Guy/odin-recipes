Jack-O-Lantern Pumpkin Pancakes

Description

The perfect breakfast to start off your spooky halloween day with the family. Set the mood for the day with these delicious pancakes, or have them as an extra special treat for Halloween desert.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of quick cooking oats
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ¾ cup canned pumpkin
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ¾ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, oats, brown sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, cloves, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In a seperate large bowl, lightly beat together the milk, egg, pumpkin, and oil.
  3. Stir flour mixture into the pumpkin mixture, blending just until moistened.
  4. Heat a lightly greased griddle over medium high heat
  5. Pour batter, ⅓ cup at a time, onto the prepared griddle. Make a jack-o-lantern face in each pancake with the chocolate chips. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 5 minutes per side.
